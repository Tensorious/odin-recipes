Martabak Telur

Description

Delicious Javanese style martabak telor / telur stuffed with savory beef filling and served with a spicy tangy sauce. Recipe for easy no-knead martabak wrappers is included.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 2 tablespoon of flour
  • 1 tablespoon tapioca flour
  • 1 tablespoon of rice flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 300 ml of water

Stuff

  • 3 Chicken eggs
  • 3 strands of leeks
  • 1 teaspoon kare powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of powder mushroom broth

Steps

  1. Mix all skin ingredients while dissolved with water until they reach sufficient thickness.
  2. Beat the eggs. Mix all stuffing ingredients. Mix well.
  3. Heat the teflon smeared with a little cooking oil.
  4. Pour 1 vegetable spoon of skin mixture, shake the Teflon until the mixture spreads to the surface of the Teflon.
  5. Add 3 tablespoons of the filling mixture to the surface of the skin mixture.
  6. Fold the martabak. Cook until both sides are half cooked. Lift and drain.
  7. Do it until all the skin ingredients and dough run out.
  8. Heat large amounts of oil on Teflon.
  9. Fry back the telfon until brownish yellow. Lift and drain.
